Brighton's Music Scene

From the narrow lanes of the city center to the seafront, Brighton has been a hub for live music since the 1960s. The city's venues have hosted everyone from The Rolling Stones and David Bowie to contemporary stars like Ed Sheeran and Florence + The Machine before they made it big. Today's Brighton music scene continues this legacy, with venues supporting both international touring artists and the vibrant local music community.
